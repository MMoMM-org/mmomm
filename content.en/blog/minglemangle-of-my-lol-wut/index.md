A lot of people ask me what is behind Mingle Mangle of My Mind (MMoMM).

It is a translation from the german word "Kuddel Muddel" and "Meine Gedanken", which is My Mind. So it might not be 100% correct but I think it fits. As an abbreviation I use MMoMM or as that is taken a lot of the time MMoMM.org.

The Mingle Mangle was one of the reasons to start digging deeper into `#PKM` and `#Obsidian`. The Mingle Mangle, all the things tossed together, the 1005 ideas which I have, trying to make them productive and structured.

The blog will grow over time with content about `#PKM`, `#Obsidian` and `#MiYo` plus some other tidbits I find interesting. And there is also my [Youtube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCl8sNtPzwDmohK-KKKw9JuA).
